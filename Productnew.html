DECO COOL GUARD

TWO COMPONENT WATERPROOF CUM INSULATION COATING

ABOUT COOL GUARD


DECO COOL GUARD is a two component acrylic modified cementitious coating that requires only on site mixing forming the ideal product to waterproof and insulate concrete, masonry, and most other construction materials. Fluid applied, DECO COOL GUARD provides a hardwearing, seamless, waterproof and insulating membrane for roofs.


Advantages:

A 1mm coating provides anti carbonation cover equivalent to over 80cm of concrete.
Gives Insulation properties. Reduces the surface temperature by 10 - 15 degrees.
Aesthetic looks. Available in White color.
Suitable for light pedestrian traffic.
Unlike conventional coatings which require a 7-28 day cure of concrete, DECO COOL GUARD can be applied to 24 hour-old concrete thereby giving immediate protection.

Product Details:

To insulate all sorts of cementitious surfaces.
As a waterproof lining for water retaining structures.
As a waterproof coating for roofs.
As a backing to marble and granite to prevent water ingress and thus alleviate surface staining.
To provide protection to concrete surfaces from carbonation and chloride attack.
